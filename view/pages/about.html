FAQs

How it works

To download the Scoot app, search “Scoot” in both the App and Google Play stores. Or click the link at the bottom of this page to be redirected to the correct page.

Can I find a nearby Scoot?

Yes, you can! The app shows the nearest Scoot to your location, so you can get one quickly.

Do I need a license to ride?

Each city has its own laws. But we recommend always wearing a helmet for your safety, regardless of local regulations.

Should I wear a helmet?

Yes! While some cities have different laws, we strongly recommend wearing a helmet every time you ride.

What about the rules & regulations?

Please check local laws before riding, but we always encourage safe and respectful riding practices.

What if I damage my Scoot?

If you damage the Scoot, contact our customer service for instructions on how to proceed.
